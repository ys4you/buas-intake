\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md4}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{About}{About}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md4}
BUAS Intake is a C++ template designed especially for students of Breda University. This template is a fork of \href{https://github.com/jbikker/tmpl8}{\texttt{ jbikker/tmpl8}} and is released into the public domain, so feel free to use and modify it as you see fit.

\begin{quote}
{\bfseries{Note\+:}} Please refer to the \href{_ getting started.pdf}{\texttt{ \+\_\+getting started.\+pdf}} document for detailed instructions. \end{quote}

\item {\bfseries{Modular Design\+:}} Organized project structure to help you get started quickly.
